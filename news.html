The 2025 Club Challenge saw Racqueteers travel to Rugby College Sports Centre — and both our teams gave everything on court.

Both our U13 and U17 teams competed in a full series of matches against Jubilee Juniors and Rugby Youth Badminton Squad. Each match consisted of 10 games — boys' singles, girls' singles, boys' doubles, girls' doubles, and mixed doubles. The standard across all four clubs was genuinely impressive, making every result feel hard-earned from the first shuttle to the last.

"Both squads showed enormous heart — every game was contested to the very last point."

Racqueteers Coaching Team

Our U17 side took gold, topping the standings with a commanding performance across all their match-ups — a result that reflects months of hard training and real improvement in team coordination. The U13s secured silver, running Rugby Youth very close and finishing ahead of Jubilee Juniors in a tightly contested day of play.

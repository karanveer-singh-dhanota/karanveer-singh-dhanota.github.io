The 7th of July saw Jubilee Juniors host another fantastic Team Challenge — and our players delivered.

Both our U13 and U17 teams competed in a series of matches against Jubilee Juniors and Rugby Youth. Each match consisted of 10 games — boys' singles, girls' singles, boys' doubles, girls' doubles, and mixed doubles. The competition was fierce, with some extraordinarily close games throughout the day.

"The competition was fierce, with some very close games — but our players rose to every challenge."

Coaching Team

This year, we were thrilled to have such talented teams representing the club. The standard of play across all three clubs was genuinely impressive, making every result feel hard-earned.

Our U13 team absolutely dominated, claiming gold with an impressive 10 wins against Jubilee Juniors and 8 wins against Rugby Youth. A stunning performance from start to finish.

While our U17 team didn't quite secure the top spot, they still put up a fantastic performance earning silver — 6 games won against Rugby Youth and a gutsy draw with Jubilee Juniors.

We extend our gratitude to our team managers and coaching team for their unwavering support throughout the day. Their guidance and encouragement played a crucial role in both teams' success.

A massive well done to all our junior players who participated in the Team Challenge. Your dedication, skill, and sportsmanship were on full display — every single one of you made the club proud.

The Team Challenge was a memorable event that showcased the talent and spirit of our young badminton juniors. We look forward to another exciting competition next year!
